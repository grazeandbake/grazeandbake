Who we are

Handcrafted in Hutton, Essex.

We're Josh & Lauren, the couple behind Graze & Bake. We create beautifully presented food for celebrations, corporate events, gatherings and special occasions across Essex and beyond.

From showstopping grazing tables and luxury platters to personalised kids' party boxes, cakes and sweet treats, everything we create is prepared with care and a real eye for detail.

We believe food should look every bit as good as it tastes. That's why every table, platter, box and bake is thoughtfully put together to make your occasion feel that little bit more special.

Beautifully made. Thoughtfully styled. Made to be shared.

As seen at events for Jacqueline Jossa & Ferne McCann — and at parties, weddings and corporate events across Essex & London.
